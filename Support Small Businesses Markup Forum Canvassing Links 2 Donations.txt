## Places to donate that will give distribute funds to businesses in need:

California:

* [Little Tokyo in Los Angeles](https://www.ltsc.org/bettertogether/) is cleaning up after protests and looting. The Little Tokyo Service Center in Los Angeles is taking [donations](https://www.ltsc.org/bettertogether/) to support it’s COVID-19 relief efforts for struggling businesses.
* [Cambodia Town](https://www.gofundme.com/f/cambodia-town-relief-fund?utm_source=customer&utm_medium=copy_link-tip&utm_campaign=p_cp+share-sheet) is a community strip located in Long Beach, California that had seen a peaceful protest turned violent. A community organizer stepped up to create a fundraising page to help rebuild the community.
* Oakland Chinatown Chamber of Commerce is [taking donations](https://www.oaklandchinatownchamber.org/) to redistribute funds to its over 150 members and families in the community in need.
* Community organizers are [seeking monetary support](https://www.gofundme.com/f/HelpSFChinatown?teamInvite=8rZnpQMnVCVXA2DPMhPRogMV9i2PDZaeF8b1SQge1YwglyUNztuOCIsAvHP8UkKV&fbclid=IwAR20WWyVth3FPu2oMkeiYFYjpJelNFG1QSXKgMaqldZvDnXYhPfvAOxmJMY) for San Francisco Chinatown where funds will be redistributed to pay for EMT, medical bills, property damage and stolen equipment.
